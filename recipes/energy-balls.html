Chocolate Protein Energy Balls

Description

Ingredients

  • 96 g Peanut Butter
  • 30 ml Honey
  • 30 g Skyr
  • 45 g Chocolate Protein Powder
  • 80 g Rolled Oats
  • 15 g Cocoa Powder
  • 4 g Instant Coffee
  • Chocolate Chips

Steps

  1. Mix together your Peanut Butter, Honey, Skyr, and Chocolate Protein Powder
  2. Add and mix in your Rolled Oats, Cocoa Powder, and Instant Coffee
  3. Add more Skyr if they don't mix up well
  4. Shape balls and store them in the fridge
